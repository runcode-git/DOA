How to use bot DOA?

Everything is very simple! Familiarize yourself with all the points below and you will figure it out very quickly, there is nothing easier, even a child can figure it out at DOA, by luck.

1. We need to log in. Use sid login or username and password, if everything went well, you will be taken to the auction tab.

2. Select lots up to 4 that you need at the auction and press the start button. Stop, press stop.

3. If you want to place your bet number, double click on the lot in the My bet column and enter your bet and click ok.

4. Set the maximum bid on all lots, click on All max bet, enter a minimum bid of 20,000 and click ok. Or double click in the column for each lot.

5. To set a credit limit, click Limit credit. The bot will work until you have the amount of credits left on the account you saved.

6. Update win - button to update the number of lots that you won in 24 hours.

7. Donate - everything speaks for itself if you want to help in the development of the bot or just to support the trousers. Good luck in the auction and in life. thank!
